Hiking

Being with nature is something that I feel is always relaxing. Going hiking get you moving and keeps you active, is relaxing, and interesting since you never know what you might find! Whether you hike into a patch or morel mushrooms, or stumble across a pair of shed antlers, to finding an old abandoned car, every step is a new adventure to take. And it's something you'll never want to miss out on.
