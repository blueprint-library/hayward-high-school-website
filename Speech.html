Speech

The speech class I took was online through WITC for college credit. It was kind of interesting to take a speech class online and watch other people's speeches. What we did in this class was create about seven different speeches.To prepare for them, we read the chapters out of our textbook, as well as did outlines and notecards. Once this was complete, we needed to have a live audience of at least four adults, so they could complete a feedback form, in which you would show at the beginning and end of the recording of your speech. Afterwards, we would always have another person's speech to look over and give feedback to as well.
